De Drie Leliën offers small-scale, shared workspaces in central Haarlem

Corona-proof 1.5m

Your new office in the Burgwal neighbourhood

In the historic home of the famous brewery De Drie Leliën, we offer shared workspaces for entrepreneurs and ZZP-ers (independent contractors). Although the building dates from the 16th century, it was fully remodeled in 2019, offering modern amenities includign air conditioning, a kitchenette with dish washer, a separated lunch and meeting space, Corona-proof seperated desks, and of course a restroom. The bright office houses 4 desks, currently home to a researcher and an online entrepreneur. Are you interested in joining us?

Spaarnwouderstraat

Our office is located at the Spaarwouderstraat 37 in the Burgwal neighborhood, a 5 minute walk from the Grote Markt and 18 minutes from Haarlem train station. There is ample parking in our street.

Your desk

You are assigned a fixed ergonomicly, height adjustable desk. Wired and wireless internet are included and can use one of our available office chairs.

Meeting and lunching

We offer a separate room that you can use to receive clients. This room is also used for our lunches and (when Corona allows them again) our "borrels".

Together yet on your own

You became an entrepreneur because you liked your independence and we fully support you in that. But by adding some office mates, we add the inspiration and social contacts that are lacking in a work from home environment.
